Delete your Café Cal account

Café Cal — a nutrition and meal tracking app developed by Nandha Ilangovan.

You can delete your Café Cal account and all associated data at any time. The fastest way is from inside the app; if you can't access the app, you can email us instead.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Café Cal on your Android device and sign in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. If you signed in with email and password, you'll be asked to re-enter your password. Google Sign-In users confirm directly.
  5. Your account and data are deleted immediately and you are signed out.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send an email from the address tied to your Café Cal account to inandha97@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Café Cal account". We will verify your account ownership and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What is deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently removed:

  • Your authentication record (email, display name, sign-in provider)
  • Your profile (name, date of birth, gender, height, weight, activity level, goals, preferences)
  • All meal entries and meal photos
  • Daily nutrition logs and streak history
  • Saved foods library
  • Weight history
  • Profile photo
  • Local app settings stored on the device

What is retained

  • Anonymous crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics) tied to a device-level install ID — not to your account — retained up to 90 days to help us fix bugs.
  • Aggregated, non-identifying analytics events (Firebase Analytics) retained according to Firebase's default retention period (up to 14 months). These cannot be linked back to you after account deletion.
  • Records we are legally required to retain (we do not anticipate any for routine accounts).
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you want a copy of your data before deletion, email inandha97@gmail.com and we'll send it in a structured format (CSV or JSON).
