Account & data

Delete your account.

You can delete your Café Cal account and all associated data at any time. The fastest way is from inside the app.

Last updated April 26, 2026

Option 1 — From inside the app

Recommended. Takes under a minute.

  1. Open Café Cal on your Android device and sign in.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. Email-and-password users re-enter their password; Google Sign-In users confirm directly.
  5. Your account and data are deleted immediately, and you are signed out.

Option 2 — By email

For when you can't access the app.

Send an email from the address tied to your Café Cal account to inandha97@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Café Cal account". We'll verify ownership and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What happens to your data

Permanently deleted

  • Authentication record (email, display name, sign-in provider)
  • Profile (name, date of birth, gender, height, weight, activity, goals, preferences)
  • All meal entries and meal photos
  • Daily nutrition logs and streak history
  • Saved foods library
  • Weight history
  • Profile photo
  • Local app settings stored on the device

Retained briefly

  • Anonymous crash diagnostics (Firebase Crashlytics) tied to a device install ID — not to your account — kept up to 90 days to help fix bugs.
  • Aggregated analytics (Firebase Analytics) retained per Firebase's default — up to 14 months. These cannot be linked back to you after deletion.
  • Records we are legally required to retain (none anticipated for routine accounts).
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you'd like a copy of your data first, email inandha97@gmail.com and we'll send it as CSV or JSON.
