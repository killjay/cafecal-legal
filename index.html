What it does

Designed to disappear into your day.

Three things, done well. No streaks-anxiety. No 47-tab settings page.

Snap a meal

Take a photo and Café Cal estimates calories and macros for you. Tap to refine. Done in seconds.

See your day

One screen, four rings — calories, protein, carbs, fats. Color-coded so you can read it at a glance.

Build the habit

Gentle streaks, weekly insights, and Thali tiers reward consistency — never harass you when you slip.

Privacy

Your data is yours.

Café Cal stores only what's needed to make the app work, and gives you full control to leave whenever you want.

One sign-in

Sign in with Google or email. We don't sell or share your data — ever.

Anonymous diagnostics

Crash reports use a device install ID, not your account. Kept up to 90 days.

Delete anytime

Remove your account and data in one tap from inside the app, or by email.
